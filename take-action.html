Take Action

You Can Help Out!

The National Women's Law Center provides quick and easy ways to contact the congress and your representatives. It includes a pre-written message for you to send. This website contains great causes to support in forms of gender equality. Please take some time to fill out some of the forms to voice the opinions that need to be heard.

Action List

Once you have completed an item, click it to check it off your list!

  • Advocate for gender equality on social media.
  • Support amazing women through their businesses and arts.
  • Call or email your state representatives. Find the contact info here or go to the National Women's Law Center page.
